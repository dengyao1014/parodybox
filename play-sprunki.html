How to Play Sprunki Incredibox

  1. Choose Your Characters: Select from our vibrant Sprunki crew. Each character contributes unique sounds to your mix.
  2. Create Your Mix: Drag sound icons onto characters to assign them sounds. Watch as they animate and perform!
  3. Experiment: Try different combinations to create your desired musical piece.
  4. Discover Bonuses: Unlock special animations through certain sound combinations.
  5. Save and Share: Once you've created a mix you love, save it and share it with the community!

Remember, there's no right or wrong way to play. Let your creativity flow and have fun!
